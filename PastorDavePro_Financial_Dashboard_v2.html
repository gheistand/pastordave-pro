💰 Tax Planning — Set Aside 25-30% of Every Profitable Dollar

As an Illinois LLC (single-member), all profit flows to your personal return. Federal self-employment tax: 15.3% on net income. Illinois state income tax: 4.95%. Combined effective rate: ~22-25% on net profit after deductions.

Rule: For every $100 of profit, move $25-30 to a separate tax savings account immediately. Quarterly estimated payments due: Apr 15, Jun 15, Sep 15, Jan 15.

Example: At 10 churches + 50 PP (~$1,200/mo net before tax) → reserve $300/mo → take home $900/mo.

✅ Is This Worth Launching? Yes — with eyes open. True break-even (all costs) = 2 churches + 10 Personal Pro users (~$448/mo revenue at $149 Church Starter pricing). At 5 churches + 25 PP = ~$600/mo after-tax income. At 15+ churches = real side-business income. CTS/Shepherd's Staff partnership skips the entire growth curve — 50 churches overnight = $5K-10K/mo gross, ~$3K-7K/mo after tax. Key action: charge NHC now, get insurance before launch, pursue CTS actively.
