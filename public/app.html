About Pastor Dave

Pastor Dave is an AI-powered pastoral companion designed to help you grow in your faith between Sundays. He is grounded in Scripture, trained on your church's sermons and teaching, and shaped by Wesleyan theology.

Pastor Dave is designed to support your pastor, not replace them. He can help you understand Scripture, reflect on sermons, follow a Bible reading plan, and think through questions of faith.

For urgent personal matters, mental health crises, or situations requiring real pastoral care, please contact your pastor or a licensed counselor directly.
