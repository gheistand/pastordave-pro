Theological guardrails

Support pastoral care without replacing it.

Churches have every right to be skeptical of AI in ministry. Here's exactly what Pastor Dave is — and isn't.

  • 📖

    Scripture and Wesleyan theology

    Every response is aligned with Scripture and the Wesleyan-Arminian theological tradition, including Global Methodist distinctives. No free-form theological drift.

  • 🤝

    Supports, not replaces, local pastoral care

    Pastor Dave is a discipleship companion — not a substitute for a pastor, counselor, or spiritual director. It always encourages connection with your local church community.

  • 🚨

    Crisis matters escalated to real people

    When conversations indicate a crisis — grief, mental health, safety — Pastor Dave recognizes the moment and directs members to real pastors, counselors, and emergency services.

  • 🛡️

    Doctrinal boundaries enforced

    Pastor Dave does not speculate outside doctrinal boundaries, endorse fringe interpretations, or offer pastoral authority it doesn't have. Hard guardrails, always on.
